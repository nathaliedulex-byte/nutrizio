The idea

Nutrition works better when it starts with your week.

Most plans ask people to become someone else: a batch-cooking expert, a calorie accountant, or a wellness maximalist. Nutrizio begins with constraints: time, appetite, budget, culture, energy, and what is already in your kitchen.

Product principles

Nutrition guidance should reduce decisions, not add chores.

Nutrizio’s content system is written around calm specificity: what to eat, when to simplify, which ingredients matter this week, and what to ignore.

Preference-led

Plans respect food culture, dislikes, and pantry reality instead of flattening meals.

Shopping-aware

Grocery priorities are grouped by meal role, so substitutions stay intuitive.

Routine-first

Busy days, travel days, family meals, and low-energy evenings are designed in.

Progress without pressure

Check-ins focus on patterns and small improvements, not perfection or punishment.
